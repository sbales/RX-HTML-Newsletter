Tech Tips

Email Signatures

Think of your email signature as a digital business card. You want it to look professional and show our corporate culture. If you haven’t updated your email signature lately or you aren’t sure if it meets brand standards, you can edit it by clicking “File” in Outlook > Options > Mail > Signatures. Make sure your default signature is selected for both new messages and replies/forwards. Click “OK”.

Michelle Dias, VP, Account Management & Marketing

Also, please don’t forget to restart your computers at the end of every shift.
